The Rebel Alliance

Common Fighters Used by the Rebels

X-Wing

Arguably the most iconic spaceship in Star Wars, the X-Wing can do it all. It comes with a shield, proton torpedo launchers, a hyperdrive, and an astromech droid. It's survivability made it a great choice for the rebellion, which was always running short on resources.

Y-Wing

The Y-Wing was a slow, dedicated bomber. It had heavy shields and armor to help fly through the dogfights to get to strategic targets. It also came with a hyperdrive, allowing it to travel independently from its capital ship. This bomber was a great compliment to the X-Wing, able to take on Star Destroyers and space stations.

A-Wing

As the most nimble of the Alliance starfighters, the A-Wing was built to fight TIEs. It came equip with shields and laser cannons, which could tear through the unshielded TIE fighters. These fighters only joined the war near the end, but they still played a critical role in destroying the second Death Star and ending the war.

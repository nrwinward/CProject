The Galatic Empire

Common Fighters Used by the Imperials

TIE Fighter

The TIE Fighter was the most common starfighter the Empire would use. Like most other TIE series fighters they traded hyperdrives and shields for maneuverability. Casualties among pilots of TIE fighters was often high, but the Empire never ran out of willing pilots to fight the Rebel Scum.

TIE Bomber

TIE Bombers were rarely used in dogfights. Their larger design made them too slow for the fast-paced combat. However, their design allowed them to carry far more fire power. Because of this firepower, they were often tasked to attack capital ships or bomb a planet into submission.

TIE Interceptor

The the TIE Interceptor builds upon the the already fast design of the TIE Fighter and makes it even faster and adds more firepower. It still does not have any shields making it easy to destroy. But with a skilled pilot at the controls, this fighter becomes a menace in any fight.

Starfighters of the Galaxy

Guide to the Starfighters of the Galaxy

Welcome to an overview of the starfighters that made up the Imperial and Rebellion navies. These nimble fighters were present in almost every space battle. The Empire used their TIE fighters to instill terror in those that would oppose them, while the Rebellion could never have won without the X-wings. This website is here to show you the variety of ways starfighters were used as well as provide specifics on individual starfighters.

Everyday Uses

While you often think of these small fighters taking on the Death Star, that was not their only use. These fighter were used to escort transports, merchant ships, and political vessels. They could also be used as personal transportation or could be flown just to take a joy ride. Some even came with an astromech droid that could do the hard computations needed for hyperspace travel for you!
